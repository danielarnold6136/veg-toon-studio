6 · Story Creation Guide 2.0 ready

The go-forward rulebook for new scripts: keep our characters consistent, but use fresh modern situations (phones, reels, online shopping, school pressure, show-off, and more), let the length fit the story (6–8 min, always >5), and follow a retention structure that keeps viewers hooked to the end. It's based on a study of how competitors hold 8–9 minute videos at 100k–4M+ views — the key finding: long videos win by packing in more story beats, not slower scenes. → 📝 Story Creation Guide 2.0
