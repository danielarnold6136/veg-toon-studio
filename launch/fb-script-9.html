9. The Reserved Seat

रिज़र्व सीट

TAG / COMMENT32s · 5 shots57 Hindi wordsNO MORAL

On a long train journey a sharply dressed man refuses to let a tired, mud-stained old farmer sit beside him, sneering that the seat is for gentlemen. At his stop he goes to meet his prospective bride's family — and the farmer is her father.

Why a scroller stops: A well-dressed man blocking the empty seat beside him so a weary old farmer has to stand — and you can sense a station up ahead where it all turns around.
Emotion arc: class snobbery → a familiar doorway → mortification

Shimla शिमला · the snobbish travellerAdrak अदरक · the farmer, the bride's fatherSeb सेब · the prospective bride
