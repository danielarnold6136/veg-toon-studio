Facebook & Instagram — setup

Starting from zero: one personal Facebook account in your real name, then the Page on top of it. New Instagram on the YouTube phone and email. Everything you need is on this page.

Do this

1

Create one personal Facebook account, using your real name — the one on your ID. You can't make a Page without a personal account behind it, and new accounts often get asked to verify with ID. Never name it "Veggie Toons": brand-named profiles get locked. Only ever make one.

2

From it, create a Page called Veggie Toons TV, and set the URL to facebook.com/veggietoonstv. It's free. (/veggietoons is taken by someone else.) Nobody can see who runs a Page — your name stays private.

3

Create the Instagram account on the YouTube phone + email. Try @veggietoonstv first. Do not sign up "with Facebook".

4

In Instagram settings, switch to a Professional account. Creator or Business — either is fine, they unlock the same things.

5

On the Page: Professional Dashboard → Monetization → Content Monetization → express interest. Free, takes a minute. Do it on day one.

Note: Instagram @veggietoons_tv is already taken by someone else — name "Veggie Toons", 2 followers, 2 posts. Not yours. Use @veggietoonstv.

Three rules — break these and you lose reach

  • Cover the Veo 3 watermark before posting anywhere on Meta. Instagram and Facebook both cut your reach for another app's watermark. Our animated badge exists for this. Our own logo is fine — it's their mark that hurts.
  • Instagram Reels: 3 minutes maximum. Anything longer is not shown to people who don't follow you — and at zero followers that's your only audience. Facebook has no limit, so the full story goes there.
  • Never add black bars to make a video vertical. Instagram demotes bordered reels. Reframe it properly or don't post it.

Money

CMT (Content Monetization Tool) is the nickname for Facebook Content Monetization. Anyone can click express interest — do it on day one. But earning starts only when Meta invites you, and they publish no follower number. India is eligible.

Your first real income is Facebook Stars — 500 followers, held 30 days. That's the target.

Instagram pays nothing per view. No ad revenue share exists there. Use it to grow and to push people to YouTube.
