8. The Division

An ailing old mother rests in the inner room while her three grown children, in the front room, argue over who is stuck with her and who gets the house and the gold. She hears every word — and quietly reaches for a pen.

Why a scroller stops: Three grown children haggling over who has to 'keep' their mother, while she listens from the next room — and then picks up a pen. You need to know what she writes.
Emotion arc: neglect → a mother overhears → a quiet decision
