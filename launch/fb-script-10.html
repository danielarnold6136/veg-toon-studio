10. At the Gate

फ़ाटक पर

SERIAL · PART 138s · 5 shots55 Hindi wordsOVERLAY: PART 1END: COMMENT FOR NEXT PARTNO MORAL

A poor man walks a long way to a grand mansion to ask his rich childhood friend for help. The friend recognises him — and, for the watching guests, coldly pretends he doesn't. Then a small one runs out of the house.

Why a scroller stops: A rich man looking his oldest friend in the eye at his own gate and saying 'I don't know you' — and just as the poor man turns to leave, a little one runs out calling him uncle.
Emotion arc: hope → a friend's denial → a small voice that changes everything

Aloo आलू · the poor childhood friendKaddu कद्दू · the wealthy friendKheera खीरा · the mansion gatekeeperMatar मटर · the wealthy man's little one
