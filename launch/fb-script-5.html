5. The Tea-Seller's Chair

चाय वाले की कुर्सी

KARMA FLIP32s · 5 shots61 Hindi wordsNO MORAL

An arrogant young candidate rushing to a big interview is rude to a plain old man sharing a bench at a roadside tea stall. Minutes later he walks into the interview room — and the chairman behind the desk is that same old man.

Why a scroller stops: A sharp-suited young man shooing a simple old man off a tea-stall bench — you can feel the comeuppance coming and you wait for it.
Emotion arc: arrogance → a familiar face → the sinking floor

Shimla शिमला · the proud candidateAloo आलू · the old man who is the chairman
