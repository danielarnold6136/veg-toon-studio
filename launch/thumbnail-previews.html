Please read — what these are (and aren't)

These are quick AI previews from a free image model, made only to show you the scene-style composition and framing. They are not the final production quality. For the real thumbnails, render the full prompt (from each story's Thumbnail Pack) in your premium tool (facelessdev / Veo / Midjourney) — you'll get sharper detail, correct character designs, and cleaner results.
  • The style is exactly right: a staged story-scene (victim vs. show-off), no text baked in, reads at a glance — matching the top competitors.
  • The free model wanders a bit: on some seeds it turned the son into a human child or gave the car a face. That's why we render-LOCK in the real tool and pick a clean take. The recommended renders above are the clean ones.
  • Text is always added after, in Canva — never let the AI draw Hindi (it renders it broken). The overlay you see here is real text laid on top, exactly how you'll do it.
  • Overlay text is a suggestion — swap for any short 2–4 word Hindi hook you like; keep it clear of the faces.

Full text-free prompts to render at production quality are in each story's Thumbnail Pack: Story 9 pack · Story 10 pack. The style rules are in the Thumbnail Playbook 2.0.
