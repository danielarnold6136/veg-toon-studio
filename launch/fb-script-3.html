3. The Seven-Rupee Gajra

सात रुपये का गजरा

WARM32s · 4 shots50 Hindi wordsHAS A MORAL

Every evening a well-off girl stops at a shy flower-seller's stall and buys a single gajra, always paying far more than it costs. He always saves the prettiest one for her. Neither says why — until a rainy night reveals it.

Why a scroller stops: A rich girl and a poor flower-boy meeting at the same stall every dusk under fairy lights — a small, warm love that makes you stay to see if it survives.
Emotion arc: tenderness → a rainy test → a kindness confessed

Seb सेब · the well-off girlGajar गजर · the poor flower-seller
Moral (this is the WARM one — add as a Canva overlay in the last 4s, never render it in Veo):
छोटी सी दया सबसे बड़ी कहानी बन जाती है।

Camera: a slow, gentle medium two-shot
