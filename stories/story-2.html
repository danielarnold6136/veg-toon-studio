📜 Ready-to-shoot script · ~6.5 min

🥵 सबने कहा "बेडौल कद्दू"... फिर भीषण गर्मी में जो हुआ 😭💧

In a drought-stricken sabzi-mandi village, a poor pumpkin mocked as fat and shapeless and rejected by the girl he loves builds a desi junk-matka water-cooler that saves the dying village from a deadly heatwave, exposing the hollow rich rival whose imported gadget fails when it matters most.

Overview

🎣 The hook

We open mid-humiliation: in front of the whole mandi the rich rival hands the love-interest a fancy imported gift while publicly insulting the hero as a "useless shapeless pumpkin," and she walks away from him. The hero's heartbreak freezes on a single tear as the narrator plants "...और फिर जो हुआ, उसने पूरे गाँव को रुला दिया" — a betrayal + public-shame + curiosity-gap combo in the first seconds so the viewer cannot scroll.

🖼️ Thumbnail concept

Split-frame 16:9. LEFT: the chubby orange pumpkin hero sitting alone in cracked dry mud, one big teardrop, head hung low, dusty heat-haze. RIGHT (smaller inset): the same pumpkin standing tall and proud beside a glowing blue desi matka-cooler with happy villagers crowding around, water mist sparkling. Big bold Hindi overlay text top: "सबने ठुकराया 😭" and bottom: "फिर हुनर ने पलट दी किस्मत! 💧🔥". Red arrow pointing from sad-side to proud-side. Bright contrasty colors, shocked emoji 😱 in corner.

🪔 Moral / सीख

असली कीमत दिखावे में नहीं, हुनर और सच्चे दिल में होती है। जो दूसरों का दर्द समझे, असली अमीर तो वही है।

Distinctness: Fresh cast never used in story-1 or the reference channels: hero is a pumpkin (कद्दू, NOT karela/bitter-gourd), love-interest is a carrot (गाजर, NOT grape/pomegranate), rival is a red capsicum (शिमला मिर्च), with supporting okra elder, pea child, and potato headman. The specific conflict — a drought/heatwave water crisis solved by a desi junk-matka evaporative cooler / water-park while the rival's imported gadget literally fails because he is hollow and rotting inside — is an original plot. We keep only the universal emotional formula (class envy, love betrayed, public humiliation, comeback, karma, honesty wins) and never reuse any specific character trio, names, gags, or storyline from the source channels.
🎵 Music arc: The arc opens on a single heartbreak stinger, then warms into gentle harmonium-and-acoustic for the setup and budding romance. It darkens through the rival's flashy entrance and public rejection into low sorrowful sarangi at rock-bottom, spikes with tense heatwave stingers and a driving dholak build-and-work montage, peaks in a triumphant uplifting percussion swell at the rescue and village celebration, lands the villain's exposure on a hollow descending sting, and resolves into a warm, swelling harmonium-flute finale that lets the hero's moral ring out. → BGM Guide

Character Design Sheet

Lock these looks before generating anything. Each design is written to be redrawn identically in every scene.

🦸
कुन्दन कद्दू (Kundan the Pumpkin)
hero

Personality: Poor, big-hearted, humble and endlessly hardworking. Mocked for his round shapeless body but secretly a clever desi-jugaad inventor who tinkers with junk. Shy around his crush, never bitter, kind even to those who mock him.

Look: A plump round bright-orange pumpkin with deep vertical ribs/grooves, a short curly green stalk on top like a little tuft of hair, big warm honey-brown expressive eyes with thick lashes, soft rounded cheeks, tiny stubby arms and legs. Wears a patched faded blue half-sleeve kurta with a visible stitched repair patch on the chest and a thin frayed brown rope belt; a small worn pencil tucked behind his stalk and a tiny cloth tool-pouch tied at his waist. Always slightly dusty. Distinguishing mark: a small heart-shaped freckle-dot on his left cheek.

Voice: Warm mid-low male voice, soft-grained and gentle, age ~22, slightly husky with a tired-but-kind texture; earnest small-town Hindi accent, calm steady pacing, rises with quiet determination, never shouts. The emotional anchor — always sincere.

💚
गुलाबो गाजर (Gulabo the Carrot)
love-interest

Personality: Sweet but initially dazzled by wealth and show; soft-hearted underneath, ashamed later when she realizes true worth. Caring, expressive, grows from naive to wise.

Look: A slender tall bright-orange carrot with a lush feathery green leafy top tied into a side-bun with a tiny yellow marigold flower, big sparkling dark eyes with long lashes, rosy blush cheeks, dainty arms and legs. Wears a flowing pink-and-green lehenga-style cloth wrap with small mirror-work sequins and thin glass bangles on both wrists. Distinguishing mark: a delicate pink bindi dot and a small silver nose-ring.

Voice: Light, sweet young-female voice, ~19, clear and melodic with a gentle airy lilt and soft blush warmth; bright in happy moments, turns trembling and breathy when guilty or remorseful. Higher pitch than all males.

😼
शानू शिमला (Shanu the Capsicum)
rival

Personality: Rich, arrogant, flashy and obsessed with imported gadgets and showing off; charming on the outside but literally hollow and rotting inside. Cowardly when his money fails.

Look: A glossy plump bright-red bell-pepper (capsicum) with broad showy shoulders, smug half-lidded eyes, a thin smirk, slick upturned green stalk styled like gelled hair. Wears a shiny golden silk waistcoat with fake gold buttons, oversized dark sunglasses pushed up on his stalk, a thick fake-gold chain, and pointy mirror-polished shoes. Carries a flashy chrome imported gadget. Distinguishing mark: hidden under his gloss, his underside has a faint dark rotting bruise-spot that shows when he panics.

Voice: Smug nasal mid-high male voice, ~25, glossy and oily with a sing-song boastful swagger and a sneering edge; loud and theatrical when showing off, but cracks into a thin panicky higher pitch when exposed. Distinctly slimy.

🥕
दादी भिंडी (Grandma Bhindi (Okra))
supporting

Personality: Frail, wise, beloved village elder; the emotional gut-punch character who nearly collapses from the heat. Represents the suffering village and the heart the hero saves.

Look: A thin, slightly bent elderly okra (lady-finger / bhindi), pale ridged light-green skin with age-spots and soft wrinkles, gentle cloudy kind eyes, tiny round spectacles, a small white cotton head-shawl. Wears a simple off-white cotton saree-wrap, walks with a tiny wooden cane. Frail and trembling in the heat. Distinguishing mark: round spectacles and a faded red bindi.

Voice: Frail, quavering elderly-female voice, ~75, thin and breathy with a slow trembling wobble and warm grandmotherly tenderness; weak and gasping in the heat, swelling with gentle wise blessing at the end.

🥕
छुटकू मटर (Chhutku the Pea)
supporting

Personality: Tiny, cheerful, loyal child who believes in the hero when no one else does; comic relief and the hero's only friend, also a heat-victim the hero rushes to cool.

Look: A very small round bright-green pea (single pea pod child) with two tiny sprout-leaves on top like ponytails, huge round innocent eyes, chubby cheeks, a wide gap-toothed grin. Wears a tiny yellow half-pant with one suspender strap, barefoot. Distinguishing mark: a little leaf-bandage on one knee.

Voice: Tiny squeaky high-pitched child voice, ~6, bubbly, fast and gap-toothed-cute with bouncy excitement and innocent wonder; the comic-relief loyal sidekick, always energetic.

🥕
सेठ आलू (Seth Aloo (Potato))
supporting

Personality: The pompous mandi headman/merchant who first ridicules the hero and later publicly eats his words; represents fickle public opinion.

Look: A large lumpy brown potato with several eyes/sprout-bumps, a thick curled grey moustache, pompous puffed chest, small greedy eyes. Wears a cream Nehru-jacket stretched over his belly, a maroon turban (pagdi), and holds a brass money-pouch. Distinguishing mark: gold-rimmed monocle on one eye.

Voice: Pompous booming heavy male voice, ~55, thick and round with a self-important rumble and a pampered drawl; fawning and grand when flattering the rich, comically panicked and wheezy in the heat, then humbled and soft when apologizing.
