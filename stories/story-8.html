Makku, a poor but brilliant corn who makes wonderful toys from scrap, is betrayed by his rich best friend Shimla who steals his secret toy invention and rises to fame on it. Cast aside and publicly humiliated, Makku rebuilds with pure honesty and skill. At a grand public toy fair the truth explodes out — the friend's empire was built on the stolen idea — karma lands, the betrayer breaks down in regret, and Makku rises as the biggest toymaker while speaking the moral of true friendship and honest hard work.

Overview

🎣 The hook

COLD-OPEN SHOCK: Open mid-betrayal — a glittering stage, a crowd cheering "Shimla! Shimla!", and rich capsicum Shimla holding up Makku's spinning wooden toy as HIS OWN invention, while poor Makku stands at the back, tears in his big eyes, whispering "ye to mera khilona hai..." The audience has no idea. We freeze on Makku's heartbroken face, then a hard cut to "3 mahine pehle" to show how the friendship began. This in-media-res betrayal hook front-loads the emotional gut-punch in the first 8 seconds before the algorithm can scroll away.

मक्कू मक्का (Makku Makka (Corn))
Hero — poor but brilliant scrap-toy maker (kabaad-se-khilaune desi inventor)

Personality: Warm-hearted, humble, endlessly creative and hopeful; pours love into every toy; trusting to a fault, which makes the betrayal cut deep; never bitter, rises with honesty and grit. The lovable underdog the audience roots for.

Look: A glossy 3D-Pixar corn cob character — bright golden-yellow kernels for a body with a soft sheen, a few green husk-leaves peeling back like a little jacket and one curled leaf as a cowlick; huge round expressive shiny brown eyes, tiny stubby arm-leaves and little feet; wears a patched-up brown apron with scrap tools (tiny screwdriver, thread spool) tucked in the pocket; smudges of grease on one cheek; warm friendly smile, slightly worn but adorable.

Voice: Warm, soft, slightly boyish mid-tone Hindi voice; gentle and earnest; cracks with emotion when hurt, steadies into calm quiet strength when resolved — the kind, sincere underdog voice.

🥕
शिमला मिर्च (Shimla (Bell Pepper / Capsicum))
Antagonist / best friend turned betrayer — rich, flashy veg who steals the toy idea

Personality: Charming and warm on the surface, but inwardly greedy and image-obsessed; craves fame and applause; rationalizes the betrayal as 'business'; cold when cornered, but ultimately breaks down in genuine regret when karma exposes her.

Look: A sleek glossy 3D-Pixar bell pepper — a plump shiny bright-red capsicum body with deep glossy highlights, a stylish green stem styled like swept-back hair, sharp confident eyes with long lashes; tiny limbs but draped in flashy 'rich' accessories — a little gold chain, oversized sunglasses pushed up, a tiny designer scarf; smug raised-eyebrow expression that melts into tearful collapse later; expensive sheen everywhere.

Voice: Smooth, confident, slightly sing-song and condescending mid-high Hindi voice; honeyed when flattering, icy and clipped when betraying, then trembling and broken in the regret scene.

🥕
छोटू भिंडी (Chhotu Bhindi (Okra Kid))
